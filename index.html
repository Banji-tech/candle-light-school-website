About Candle Light High School Nakasajja

Our Mission

To provide quality education that illuminates young minds and prepares students for a bright future through academic excellence, character development, and innovative learning approaches.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Uganda, producing well-rounded graduates who are academically excellent, morally upright, and ready to contribute positively to society.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Respect, and Community Service form the foundation of our educational philosophy and guide every aspect of our school culture.

Located on Gayaza Kalagi Road in the serene environment of Nakasajja, Candle Light High School has been a beacon of educational excellence. Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and comprehensive curriculum ensure that every student receives the best possible education to prepare them for higher learning and future careers.
